What's Next?

This contains what the students expect for the 10th grade level and their opinions about their ICT for this school year.


Martina Cabrillos:
For grade 10, I am expecting a challenging yet informative learning for
us students. Wherein, this may hone our skills as young leaders and enhance our knowledge
towards the world of technology. Moreover, grade 9 was admittedly challenging yet it made
my learning towards technology more diverse as we explored different approaches to coding.
It was truly worth my while despite it not being my forte.



Victor Gorom:
I just want to finish Grade 9 ICT and never touch
Javascript again. I'm expecting more stress on coding
for Grade 10 ICT since it's not HTML based anymore💔.



Cyrenne Mendez:
I expect grade 10 to be a lot harder and filled with challenges that will
leave me drained by the end of the week. My honest opinion about grade 9 is its definitely
harder than the previous levels and uses more of knowledge of the app and the codes itself.



Kirsten Santos:
ICT was a tough subject for me since it required hard work and perseverance
in coding. I expect grade 10 to be more complicated
which will possibly make my grades get lower.
